WNS Solution

With our state-of-the-art WMS Solution (Warehouse Management System) integrated into your company’s ERP system, it’s never been easier to optimize the management of your warehouse functions whenever you need. WMS Solution targets being easy and explicit to use without sacrificing the range of features needed to run a successful distribution of central operations. Key properties of WMS Solution: • shipment, order and inventory handling • human resource planning • integration with ERP and WCS • user-friendly user interface • data analytics tools

WCS Solution

Our WCS Solution (Warehouse Control System) is an ideal software resolution for controlling and monitoring your daily activities inside your warehouse units. Using latest software development features, WCS Solution ensures your unit execute processes precisely in time utilizing real-time data to optimize the functions of the unit. Key properties of WCS Solution: • operation of automated processes (e.g. conveyors, sortation systems) • data collecting • exception handling • integration with WCS
